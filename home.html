Student Tracking for the English Department


This database was designed exclusively for the English department at the University of Mississippi for the purpose of tracking student progress and gathering data on English alumni.

The application provides a user-friendly graphic interface, allowing the English faculty to visualize and access information and trends about English alumni.


To begin with data submission, click the Submit link. For querying of data, begin with the Query link. And for data visualization, navigate to the Visual page.
